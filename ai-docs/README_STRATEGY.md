# Dotflat Launch Strategy

> Complete guide to launching a commodity-backed stablecoin from $0 to $1B TVL

## 🚀 Three Ways to Start

### Option 1: Bootstrap ($50k, 12 weeks) ⭐ RECOMMENDED
**Best for**: Solo founder with code but limited capital

- [Quick Start Guide](QUICK_START.md) - Start THIS WEEK
- [Fundraising Strategy](docs/pages/fundraising-strategy.rst) - Bootstrap details
- **Result**: Launch lean, prove concept, raise later from strength

### Option 2: Raise First ($2M, 6 months)
**Best for**: Team with network and fundraising experience

- [Fundraising Strategy](docs/pages/fundraising-strategy.rst) - VC fundraising
- [Full Marketing Plan](MARKETING_STRATEGY.md) - Execute with capital
- **Result**: Fast growth, competitive advantages, but 6 months to launch

### Option 3: Hybrid ($500k, 9 months) 💡 BEST ROI
**Best for**: Founder who can raise small friends & family round

- Bootstrap first 6 months → Prove it works
- Raise $500k-1M with traction → Better terms (5% dilution vs 15%)
- Scale with capital → $5M → $50M TVL
- **Result**: Keep most equity, less risk, better valuation

Time to Launch: Faster than pure bootstrap

## 🎓 Key Insights

### What You DON'T Need to Start
- ❌ $10M in funding
- ❌ 20-person team
- ❌ Tier 1 VC backing
- ❌ Perfect product
- ❌ Big marketing budget

### What You DO Need
- ✅ $50k capital (or ability to raise it)
- ✅ Working code (you have this!)
- ✅ Security audit ($15k)
- ✅ Initial liquidity ($15k)
- ✅ 12 weeks of hustle
- ✅ Willingness to learn and iterate

### The Secret
**Prove it works FIRST, then raise capital from strength.**

This gives you:
- Better valuation (3-5x higher)
- Better terms (more founder-friendly)
- Better investors (Tier 1 VCs interested)
- More control (you choose investors)
- Less dilution (5-7% vs 15-20%)
