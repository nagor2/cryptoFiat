### 4. Risk Management & Black Swans

**Новое**: Playbooks для 4 worst-case scenarios

#### Black Swan #1: Smart Contract Exploit
```
Severity: CRITICAL
Playbook:
├─ Phase 1: Detection (0-5 min)
├─ Phase 2: Triage (5-15 min)
├─ Phase 3: Containment (15-60 min)
├─ Phase 4: Communication (15-120 min)
├─ Phase 5: Fix & Recovery (hours-days)
├─ Phase 6: Post-Mortem (48h)
└─ Phase 7: Compensation

Includes: Emergency contacts, pause mechanism, templates
```

#### Black Swan #2: Market Crash (ETH -70%)
```
Severity: HIGH
Playbook:
├─ Early Warning (days before)
├─ During Crash (circuit breakers, dynamic ratios)
├─ Stabilization (DAO vote, peg defense)
└─ Recovery (analysis, upgrades, support)

Includes: Automated responses, liquidation optimization
```

#### Black Swan #3: Regulatory Crackdown
```
Severity: HIGH
Playbook:
├─ Preparation (legal foundation, decentralization)
├─ Immediate Response (assess, strategize, communicate)
├─ Strategic Response (comply/challenge/pivot)
└─ Long-term Adaptation

Includes: 4 strategy options, timeline, budget
```

#### Black Swan #4: Competitor with $100M
```
Severity: MEDIUM
Playbook:
├─ Intelligence (monitor, analyze)
├─ Positioning (differentiate, don't compete)
├─ Execution (community moat, product velocity)
├─ Narrative (David vs Goliath)
└─ Realistic Assessment (when to pivot)

Includes: Competitive jiu-jitsu tactics
```

**Результат**: Готовы к любому кризису
