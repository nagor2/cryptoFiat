**Week 42-43: Credit Delegation**

Uncollateralized Lending::

    Concept:
    ├─ Alice has large CDP (trusted)
    ├─ Bob needs DFC (untrusted)
    ├─ Alice delegates credit to Bob
    ├─ Bob borrows without collateral
    ├─ Alice's CDP is liable
    └─ Trust-based lending

Implementation::

    Risk Parameters:
    ├─ Max delegation: 20% of CDP
    ├─ Interest rate: Market + 2%
    ├─ Alice earns spread
    ├─ Reputation system
    └─ Default handling
    
    Use Cases:
    ├─ Businesses lending to employees
    ├─ DAOs lending to members
    ├─ Friends/family lending
    └─ Institutional credit lines

Credit Scoring::

    On-Chain Reputation:
    ├─ CDP payment history
    ├─ Deposit track record
    ├─ Governance participation
    ├─ Community standing
    └─ Credit score (300-850)
    
    Benefits:
