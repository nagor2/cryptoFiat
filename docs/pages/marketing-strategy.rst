Marketing Strategy
==================

Overview
--------

This document outlines the comprehensive marketing and growth strategy for the Dotflat (DFC) project - the first commodity-backed stablecoin on Ethereum.

Unique Value Proposition
-------------------------

Core Positioning
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

**Main competitive advantage**: DFC is backed by a basket of commodities rather than USD, providing:

* 📊 **"Stablecoin protected from fiat inflation"**
* 🏆 **"Digital Gold 2.0 with commodity basket"**
* 🌍 **"Global currency independent of central banks"**

Why This Matters
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Traditional stablecoins (USDT, USDC, DAI) are:
- Exposed to USD inflation
- Dependent on banking system
- Subject to regulatory risks
- Centralized (except DAI)

DFC offers:
- Real asset backing (gold, copper, oil, etc.)
- Decentralized governance via RULE tokens
- Protection from fiat currency debasement
- Transparent on-chain reserves

    Lock RULE → Receive vRULE (voting power)
    
    Lock Duration Options:
    ├─ 1 week   → 1.00 RULE = 0.01 vRULE
    ├─ 1 month  → 1.00 RULE = 0.04 vRULE
    ├─ 3 months → 1.00 RULE = 0.12 vRULE
    ├─ 6 months → 1.00 RULE = 0.25 vRULE
    ├─ 1 year   → 1.00 RULE = 0.50 vRULE
    ├─ 2 years  → 1.00 RULE = 0.75 vRULE
    └─ 4 years  → 1.00 RULE = 1.00 vRULE (max)

**vRULE Benefits**:

1. **Governance Voting**: 1 vRULE = 1 vote
2. **Fee Sharing**: Share of protocol revenue
3. **Boost LP Rewards**: Up to 2.5x multiplier
4. **Priority Access**: New features, partnerships

**Fee Distribution**:

All protocol fees collected in DFC::

    CDP Interest Fees      → 1% of all positions
    Liquidation Penalties  → 13% of liquidated amount
    Withdrawal Fees        → 0.5% of early withdrawals

    100% distributed to vRULE holders weekly

**Expected APY for vRULE Holders**: 15-30% (fee dependent)
